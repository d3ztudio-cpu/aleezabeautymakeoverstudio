About Aleeza

Aleeza Beauty Makeover Studio was founded by Mrs. Shymi Thomas on 23 January 2019 with a vision to bring joy, confidence, and elegance to every individual who walks through the door. The studio was inaugurated by her mother and blessed by Rev. Fr. Shaji Kochupurakkal, the former Vicar of Mount Carmel Church, Mundur.

The name Aleeza, meaning joyful, beautifully reflects the spirit of our studio—where every service is delivered with passion, warmth, and a commitment to enhancing natural beauty.

SHYMI THOMAS

FOUNDER OF ALEEZA BEAUTY MAKEOVER STUDIO

Located near Sobha City Mall, Puzhakkal – Ambakkad Church Road, Thrissur, Kerala, Aleeza Beauty Makeover Studio has grown into a trusted beauty destination known for creativity, professionalism, and genuine care. The studio was renovated with a fresh new look on March 08, 2024, and expanded with the opening of a second shop at the same location on September 2, 2025.

The founder, Mrs. Shymi Thomas, is a highly accomplished beauty professional with several prestigious recognitions to her name:

  • Kalams World Record Holder for Spectacular Ramp Walk – Season 3 (Rising Stars Event)
  • National-Level Bridal Contest Winner
  • Kerala State-Level 4th Runner-Up (Bridal Contest)
  • Best Eye Makeup Award – State Level

With her dedication, talent, and artistic vision, Mrs. Shymi has built Aleeza into a space where clients experience more than just beauty services—they feel valued, confident, and celebrated. Her expertise in bridal and fashion makeover artistry has made Aleeza a preferred choice for brides, models, and clients seeking personalized beauty transformations.

At Aleeza Beauty Makeover Studio, we go beyond traditional beauty services. We focus on understanding each client's style, personality, and comfort to create looks that highlight individuality and natural charm. Our team stays updated with the latest trends, techniques, and international beauty standards, ensuring that every makeover is modern, elegant, and timeless.

We also provide professional beauty training courses for aspiring artists, helping women build confidence, develop their skills, and step into the world of beauty with knowledge and creativity. Many of our students have gone on to establish themselves successfully in the beauty industry.
